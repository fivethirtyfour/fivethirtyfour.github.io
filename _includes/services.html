Web Design

Having a quality, modern, responsive, and mobile friendly website can mean the success or failure of your company. In today's internet first, technological society it's necessary to have a presence on the internet to reach all of your customers. Statistics indicate that 53% of site visitors will immediately leave your site if it takes more than 3 seconds to load; simply having a website isn't enough, it needs to be a performant and responsive website with the right focus and stategy for your business.

Non-profit

Non-profits have a unique challenge when it comes to modernizing their organizations, cost. Technology can be expensive but there are many ways to provide enterprise level technologies at a non-profit budget. Choosing the right technologies so you can focus on your mission is can mean the difference between making payroll and making a difference.

Small Business

Small businesses employ 48% of the US's private sector employees. Of those the largest segment is businesses with 20 employees or less. Small business often can't afford to have their own IT employee handling all of their needs. With cloud technologies and web based services, having an on staff IT person isn't always needed. Hiring someone to service and manage your IT needs can save small businesses tens of thousands of dollars a year.

Enterprise E-Recyling

Some times you just need to have a safe, secure, responsible, and affordable solution for getting rid of your old electronics. With implementing Department of Defense destruction protocols you can be assured that your data won't fall into the wrong hands.

G-Suite & Office 365 Migration

Making the move from locally hosted applications to cloud based services can save you thousands of dollars each year by reducing technical support calls, expensive licensing, employee downtime, and out-dated software. Hosting your email, calendar, documents on a cloud services infrastructure like Google's G-Suite or Microsoft's Office 365 can massively improve the productivity of your organization.

Physical to Cloud Migrations

Maintaining on-premises hardware can get extremely expensive having to replace hardware every few years, upgraded expensive licenses, replacing failing hardware, updates, and technical maintenance. Having a hard drive fail on a local server could mean your company being out of commission for hours or days depending on the severity. With cloud technologies if a hard drive fails the server just moves to a different hard drive reducing unnecessary downtime and loss of productivity.
